Senior Medical Helper

‚ö†Ô∏è Important: Senior Medical Helper collects minimal personal information, and all collected data is stored only on your device and never transmitted to external servers.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Location Information

  • Purpose: To find nearby hospitals around your location
  • Collection Time: Only while using the app (real-time)
  • Storage: Temporarily stored only in device memory
  • Retention Period: Immediately deleted when app closes
  • Third-party Sharing: None (not transmitted to servers)

1.2 Information We DO NOT Collect

  • Name, social security number, date of birth, or other personal identification information
  • Phone number, email address, or other contact information
  • Medical records or health information
  • Payment information (free app)
  • User behavior patterns or search history

2. How We Use Your Information

Location information is used only for:

  • Searching for nearby medical facilities based on your current location
  • Calculating distance to medical facilities
  • Providing departure point information when connecting to map apps

3. Data Retention and Usage Period

Location information is processed as follows:

  • Storage Location: User device internal memory (RAM) only
  • Retention Period: Temporarily stored only while app is in use
  • Deletion: Immediately deleted when app closes or moves to background
  • Server Transmission: None (all processing is done locally)

4. Third-party Sharing

This app does not share collected personal information with third parties.

However, minimal information may be shared with external apps you explicitly choose:

  • Map Apps (Google Maps, Naver Map, Kakao Map): When you tap the "Directions" button, departure (current location) and destination (hospital location) coordinates are passed to the selected map app. This occurs with your explicit consent and is subject to the map app's privacy policy.
  • Phone App: When you tap the "Call" button, the hospital phone number is passed to your device's phone app.

5. Data Deletion

Since this app does not store personal information on servers, no separate deletion procedure is required.

5.1 When Uninstalling

  • All app data is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app
  • You can also delete data via: Device Settings > Apps > Senior Medical Helper > Clear Data

5.2 Revoking Location Permission

  • Device Settings > Apps > Senior Medical Helper > Permissions > Location > Disable
  • Note: Hospital search functionality will be limited without location permission

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights:

  • Deny Location Permission: You can deny location permission during installation or in device settings
  • Partial Usage: Some features (like calling hospitals) can be used without location permission
  • Uninstall: You can uninstall the app at any time to remove all data
